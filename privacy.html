Privacy Policy for iTreeView

Effective Date: 4/11/2026

iTreeView is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what data is collected, how it is used, and your rights regarding that data.

1. Information Collection

iTreeView operates locally and does not collect or transmit personal data except as required for license activation.

When activating a license, the following information may be collected:

  • Email address
  • License key
  • Machine identifier

No CAD files or design data are collected, transmitted, or stored externally.

2. Use of Data

Collected data is used strictly for:

  • License activation and validation
  • Preventing unauthorized use

No personal data is sold, rented, or used for advertising purposes.

3. Third-Party Services

iTreeView uses third-party services such as Stripe for payment processing. These services may collect and process payment-related data according to their own privacy policies.

iTreeView does not store or access payment details such as credit card information.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

License-related data is retained only as long as necessary to provide license validation and support services.

Users may request deletion of their data by contacting the email below.

5. User Rights

Users may request access to or deletion of their data at any time by contacting support.

6. Security

Reasonable measures are taken to protect transmitted data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure.

7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page.
