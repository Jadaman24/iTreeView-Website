Privacy Policy for iTreeView

Effective Date: 4/11/2026

iTreeView is committed to protecting your privacy. This application does not collect, store, or transmit personal data except as required for license activation.

Information Collection

iTreeView operates locally and only interacts with Autodesk Inventor files on your system. No CAD data is uploaded or transmitted.

License Data

When activating a license, the following may be transmitted:

  • Email address
  • License key
  • Machine identifier

This data is used only for license validation.

Third-Party Services

Payments are handled by third-party providers such as Stripe. iTreeView does not store payment information.
